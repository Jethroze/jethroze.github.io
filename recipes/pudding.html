Pudding Recipe


Description

A classic and popular Japanese dessert made with eggs, milk, and sugar.
It is also commonly known as Purin.

Ingredients

  • 200 mL of milk
  • 4 tbsp of water
  • 2 eggs
  • 7 tbsp of sugar (4 tbsp and 3 tbsp separate)

Steps

  1. Melt 4 tbsp of sugar in a sauce pan over medium heat.
  2. Once the sugar is fully melted, gradually add 4 tbsp of water into the sauce pan.
  3. Transfer the liquid into two small cups to shape the pudding for later.
  4. Crack 2 eggs into a bowl and stir. Be careful not to create bubbles.
  5. Add 3 tbsp of sugar into the bowl of eggs.
  6. Sieve the mixture twice into another bowl.
  7. Warm up 200mL of milk in a sauce pan over a low heat.
  8. With the warm milk, gradually add it into the egg mixture while stirring.
  9. Pour the milk and egg mixture into the two small cups with the melted sugar. Tightly wrap tinfoil over the mouth of the cups.
  10. Prepare a pot with water and place a paper towel at the bottom of the pot. Heat up water to a boil over medium heat.
  11. Once water starts to boil, place cups in pot (tinfoil side up) and place the lid on the pot. Cook the pudding for 8 minutes over medium heat.
  12. After 8 minutes, take out the cups and place them on a counter to cool down. Once fully cooled, move the cups to the refrigerator and leave them there for 3 hours.
  13. After 3 hours, open the cups and scrape the edge of the pudding with a toothpick then transfer to a bowl.
